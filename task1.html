Artificial Intelligence (AI):

AI simulates human intelligence in machines, enabling them to perform tasks like decision-making, learning, and problem-solving. It includes narrow AI for specific tasks and general AI for broader capabilities. Through machine learning, AI systems improve by analyzing data. AI is transforming industries like healthcare, finance, and transportation by automating processes and enhancing efficiency. However, it also raises ethical concerns, such as privacy, job displacement, and bias. Addressing these challenges is essential to ensure AI's benefits are maximized while minimizing risks.
